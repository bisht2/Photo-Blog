About

This is a space for stillness, reflection, and the small wonders that often go unnoticed.

Through photography and film, I try to capture the feeling of a place, the quality of light, the way time seems to slow when you're truly present.

Each image here holds a memory—not just of where I was, but of how it felt to be there.

Thank you for pausing here with me.
